Optional Extras and FAQs

Personal Consultation

We can arrange a virtual meeting to discuss your work in depth at a cost of 75€. This is typically held after the edit/review stage.

Test page

To ensure you are pleased with the outcome, I’m happy to edit and/or review a test page for just 5euros. If you then choose to have a full edit/review, the 5euros will be discounted from the total cost.

Turnaround time

Depending on the length of the work, the involve approximately 4-7 day turnaround.

Rush orders

Quicker turnaround times may be requested at a higher cost (50% on top of the standard fee). Please contact me directly to determine the time frame (approximately 48hours for a standard scientific article).

What is included in the word count?

The title page and reference list are not included in the word count unless their editing is specifically requested by the author. In-text citations are automatically included in the word count as these are essential to understanding the work.

How do I pay?

Payment is possible via PayPal or a bank transfer. I will provide an invoice at the commencement of the edit/review process.
