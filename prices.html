Optional Extras and FAQs

Test page

To ensure you are pleased with the outcome, I’m happy to edit and/or review a test page for just 5€. If you then choose to have a full edit/review, the 5€ will be discounted from the total cost.

Turnaround time

Depending on the length of the work, the involve approximately 4-7 day turnaround. You will be provided with a precise turnaround date when you submit your work.

Rush orders

Quicker turnaround times may be requested at a higher cost (50% on top of the standard fee). Please contact me directly to determine the time frame (approximately 48hours for a standard scientific article).

Combined Edit & Review

A discounted price of 0.08€ per word is available when selecting both Linguistic Editing and Academic Review options.

What is included in the word count in my text?

Yes! Making a text concise is a major challenge for all authors. For an additional cost, you may request that the linguistic editing includes decreasing the word count to a specific limit.

What is included in the word count?

The title page and reference list are not included in the word count unless their editing is specifically requested by the author. In-text citations are automatically included in the word count as these are essential to understanding the work.

How do I pay?

Payment is possible via PayPal or a bank transfer. I will provide an invoice when the edit/review process commences.
