Optional Extras and FAQs

Test page

A test page may be submitted for just 5€, which will be returned edited and/or reviewed. If you then choose to have a full edit and/or review, the 5€ will be discounted from the total cost.

Turnaround time

Depending on the length of the work, the turnaround time is 4-7 days. You will be provided with a precise turnaround date when you submit your work.

Rush orders

Quicker turnaround times may be requested at a higher cost (50% on top of the standard fee). Please contact me directly to establish a time frame.

Combined Edit & Review

A discounted price of 0.08€ per word is available when both Linguistic Editing and Academic Review options are requested.

Can you decrease my word count?

Yes! Making a text concise is a major challenge for all authors. For an additional cost, you may request that the Linguistic Editing include decreasing the word count to a specific limit.

How do I pay?

Payment is possible via PayPal or a bank transfer. I will provide an invoice when the edit/review process commences.
