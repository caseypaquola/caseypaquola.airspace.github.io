Personalised help for improving your scientific articles

Welcome to Approved!

Approved! offers a personalised approach to improving scientific articles and grant applications. All work is solely undertaken by me, Casey Paquola, which ensures your work is handled by an active academic.

By publishing in and reviewing for high impact journals, I’ve learnt how to craft a piece of work to maximise its potential. I can help you do the same by making your text more clear and persuasive and/or providing a constructive academic review prior to submission.

Whether writing is not your forte or English is a second language, I can help to improve the clarity of work and emphasise its importance, thereby enhancing your chance of success.
